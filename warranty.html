Warranty

  • Customer satisfaction is always prioritized at Less Used Engines. That's why we offer parts and labor warranty on Used engines. Continue reading to learn more about used engine warranty's terms and restrictions.
  • Our guarantee policy covers any defective components or embellishments that we send out. Almost every car part comes with a service agreement. Labor costs are not covered by our guarantee framework.
  • If a portion is found to be faulty, we will try to replace it first; if this is not practicable, we will refund the amount paid to your account.
  • If a reorder is placed before the underlying item arrives, we shall request immediate payment of the subsequent part plus a discount.
  • Less Used Engines is also not accountable for any expenditures incurred as a result of incorrect engine installation or labor fees. To our knowledge, the best engine is a used one with little miles. Oil leaks/damage on engines caused by non-replaced seals, gaskets, and filters are not covered by the Less Used Engines warranty.
  • We are selling second-hand engines to you. We create efficient and attractive engine parts for all vehicles, but engine appearance is not guaranteed.
  • Before signing for delivery, the customer should inspect all of the pieces. We are not responsible for any refunds related to used engines or parts that were incorrectly diagnosed.
  • Less Used Engines does not bear the costs of towing, shipping, storage, or a rental vehicle. Your warranties will be canceled if you have inaccurate details. OEM car parts from various makes and models are interchangeable.
  • Less Used Engines employs a VIN compatibility check to ensure that the used engine for sale that you have ordered is a good and perfect match for your car. Unless otherwise stated, any deposits made to us are non-refundable.
  • Less Used Engines authorizes the return or exchange of used engines. If a shipping order is returned, it should be received in the same assembled state as when it was sent to the customer.
  • If the order is not received in the same condition as it was sent for whatever reason, we will not issue a refund. Only if the replacement is not available will a refund be issued.
  • The guarantee will be invalidated if the engine has been disassembled or altered in any manner without Less Used Engines permission.
  • Our reconditioned engines are thoroughly tested, visually inspected, and cleaned.
